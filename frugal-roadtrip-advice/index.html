Frugal Roadtrip Advice

I tend to be very frugal. So when my girlfriend and I decided to go on a roadtrip after we were done with school I decided that we would do it as cheaply as possible.

One of our biggest hangups early on was the fact that we didn’t want to spend $70/night or more on a hotel or B&B. I saw on /r/frugal that some WalMarts would let you sleep in your car in their parking lots overnight, either with a RV or just a regular car.

After reading many reviews and feedback from people who have done this, it’s very hit or miss. I read on this guide to WalMart parking lot camping that the founder of WalMart has been vocal about his stores providing a safe spot for people on the road to stay overnight. Even with this, some WalMarts definitely do not let you sleep overnight in their parking lots.1

So I got to thinking, where could you park overnight and definitely not be bugged. Then it hit me, hotel parking lots! This provides a nice place to sleep during the night, lots are usually filled with many cars so you’ll just blend in. In the morning, go in and use the restroom, if it’s a nice hotel get a free cup of coffee and be on your way! I think it would be better if your car had some tinted windows in the back, at least for a small bit of privacy. In addition, you could put a foldable sunshade in the front windsheild for even more privacy.

I imagine that we’ll be using this a lot on our roadtrip. We plan on leaving the week of August 19th, 2013. During the roadtrip and some more before I’ll be sure to share any plans we have of saving some money and seeing the country!
